the fish swimming in the stone | scene 0 : about this book, "the fish swimming in the stone" |

you made it! thanks for your patience in navigating through this book.


this reactive book is an interdisciplinary work

that consists of images, poetry and sound,

and is based on the artist's childhood memories of

flipping through children's books --

those books were never short of colorful drawings that,

every moment, would stir up the artist's imagination.

so here's an artist's book that can hopefully do the same to its readers.


all images and poetry are original works by the author,

but the the artist would like to make note that

all forms of art should be a fruit of inspiration

and even appropriation from various sources.



special thanks to my mom

Haruko Murano

for her enduring love and prayers
