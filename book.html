about ReactiveZine

welcome! this responsive, multimedia zine is made with both on and offline inputs.

most importantly, it functions on the interface with its reader.

ReactiveZine uses love for art as a key ingredient :

it is intended as a self-publishing platform to distribute the love

while connecting the readers in sharing it further.


user-guide :

this multimedia book is designed to give readers sensory experiences.

please make sure that your speaker is turned on

and your mouse is in front of you before navigating the contents.


any issues or comments ? :

for any questions, issues, comments (or just to say hello)

email the artist.
